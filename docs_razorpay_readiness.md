# Razorpay International Readiness Checklist

## Dashboard and Compliance
- Complete KYC and account activation.
- Enable international payments in Razorpay dashboard.
- Confirm business category and export purpose code.
- Ensure website has policy pages and valid support contacts.

## Ops Docs
- MSA/SOW template for US clients.
- Export invoice template with service details and currency.
- Reconciliation SOP mapping payment ID to invoice and contract.

## Technical
- Webhook configured with secret and signature validation.
- Track order/payment IDs and maintain immutable audit logs.
- Store original transaction currency and INR settlement references.
